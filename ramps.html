Ramps for Delivery Trucks

One tough walk ramp

TODCO Rollaramp

The TODCO Rollaramp™ is the strong, durable walk ramp for any truck. It's designed to be tough, and to increase driver convenience and safety. It is available in a variety of standard sizes, from 8' to 16', with capacities up to 2,000 lbs. In addition, TODCO can also make custom size ramps to fit individual needs. Plus, with two different skid-resistant surfaces to choose from, in addition to two nose designs, we have a Rollaramp that matches your delivery demands.

TODCO Spring Assist

Drivers may pull and lift their walk ramp dozens of times a day. And that can result in serious driver fatigue. That's why TODCO was the first to develop the patented Spring Assist™ system of Rollaramp™ walk ramps. With the Spring Assist system, drivers can easily lift ramps to bed height, making lighter work of walk ramps.
